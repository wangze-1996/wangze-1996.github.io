## 🧑‍🔬 About Me

I am a researcher in hydrology and water resources. My work bridges traditional water sciences with cutting-edge artificial intelligence to build smarter, more resilient water systems. 

Currently, my research focuses on **AI-driven methods for hydrological sensing, prediction, and decision-making**, with a particular emphasis on:
* **Computer Vision:** Leveraging vision foundation models for hydrological monitoring.
* **Generative AI:** Developing large language models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and domain-specific AI agents for water resources applications.
* **Decision Support:** Improving flood forecasting, uncertainty analysis, and reservoir operation.

---

## 🎯 Research Interests

* 🌊 **AI for Hydrology & Water Resources** (Flood forecasting, uncertainty analysis, reservoir operation)
* 👁️ **Vision-based Sensing** (Hydrological monitoring and water level estimation using foundation models)
* 🤖 **Domain-specific AI Agents** (Large language models and RAG for water resources engineering)
* 🔄 **Interdisciplinary Methods** (Sensing, prediction, and decision support in complex water systems)
